How InboxCleaner Works

A transparent look at how our desktop application safely connects to your account and processes your data strictly on your machine.

1

Securely Connect to Gmail

Launch the application and sign in using Google's official OAuth secure login window. InboxCleaner only requests the specific permissions needed to read and manage your emails. We never see, touch, or store your actual Google password.

2

Local Machine Learning Scan

Once connected, the software downloads email headers and metadata directly to your computer's RAM. Our built-in, offline AI engine then categorizes your emails into Newsletters, Receipts, Promotions, and Spam. No data is ever sent to a third-party cloud.

3

Review & 1-Click Clean

The dashboard presents a visual summary of your inbox clutter. You have total control: review the categories, select the senders you no longer want to hear from, and hit "Clean". The app will bulk-archive or delete the emails and automatically send unsubscribe requests where possible.
